Dr. Lorenzo Nicolè - Biography

Dr. Lorenzo Nicolè is a board-certified pathologist and medical doctor specializing in oncologic pathology, AI-driven digital histopathology, and computational pathology. His research focuses on integrating artificial intelligence into cancer diagnostics and precision medicine.

Dr. Nicolè earned his Medical Degree and Pathology Board Certification from the University of Padova. He is currently working at Angelo Hospital in Mestre, where he focuses on clinical diagnostics and AI-assisted histopathology workflows.

During his career, he was fortunate to work with well-known professors from:

  • University of Padova: Prof. Paolo Angelo De Tos, Prof. Matteo Fassan, Prof. Massimo Rugge
  • University of Ferrara: Prof. Stefano Volinia
  • University of Padova - AI & Digital Pathology: Prof. Stefano Ghidoni

His research interests include Pathology, Cancer, AI-assisted Cancer Diagnostics, and Precision Medicine.
