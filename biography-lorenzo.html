Dr. Lorenzo Nicolè - Biography

Dr. Lorenzo Nicolè is a board-certified medical doctor and pathologist, specializing in oncologic pathology, digital histopathology, and AI-driven computational pathology**. His research focuses on the integration of artificial intelligence and deep learning technologies in cancer diagnostics and precision medicine.

With extensive expertise in histopathological evaluation and AI-assisted medical diagnostics, Dr. Nicolè has been actively involved in interdisciplinary collaborations that bridge pathology, computational medicine, and artificial intelligence. His work contributes to **advancing automated cancer detection systems, enhancing diagnostic accuracy, and optimizing whole slide imaging (WSI) workflows**.

Dr. Nicolè is a principal investigator in pathology and precision medicine, and his contributions extend to both clinical research and applied AI methodologies for histological tissue analysis.
