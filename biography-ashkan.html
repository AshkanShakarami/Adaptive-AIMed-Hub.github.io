Ashkan Shakarami - Biography

I am a Computer Scientist and Postdoctoral Fellow in Artificial Intelligence (AI) at the University of Bonn (Universität Bonn) and University-Hospital Bonn (Universitätsklinikum Bonn: UKB). My research interests are AI, Computer Vision, Neural Networks, Computer-Aided Diagnosis, Histopathology, and Ophthalmology.
