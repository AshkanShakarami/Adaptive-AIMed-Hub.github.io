Ashkan Shakarami - Biography

I hold a PhD degree in Artificial Intelligence. During my academic endeavor, I was fortunate to work with well-known Full Professors from:

  • Amirkabir University: Prof. Mohammad Bagher Menhaj
  • University of Padova: Prof. Stefano Ghidoni, Prof. Paolo Angelo De Tos
  • New York University: Prof. Yao Wang
  • Technical University of Munich: Prof. Nassir Navab

My expertise lies in AI-driven digital pathology, computational histopathology, and CAD systems for medical applications.
